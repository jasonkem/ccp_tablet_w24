Travel Destinations

Madrid, Spain

Last semester I studied abroad in Madrid, Spain. I lived in the La Latina neighborhood in an 8 person flat. 3 of my roommates were from Umich, 2 from Spain, one from France, and the last one from Italy. I loved my time in Spain and thought that La Latina was the perfect location for me.

Likes

  • Close Metro Stop
  • Neighborhood close to school
  • Lots of delicious coffee shops

Positano, Italy

My last visit abroad was to Positano, Italy. This was my favorite location for several reasons, as listed below. I traveled to Positano with my brother and my girlfriend. The food was amazing, and the views of the sea were stunning.

Likes

  • Amazing food!!
  • Perfect 60-80 degree weather
  • Breathtaking sunrises and sunsets

Paris, France

While abroad, my entire family visited during spring break, and we went to London and Paris. I really enjoyed Paris, a city rich in history with amazing art in its many museums.

Likes

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Plenty of shopping options
  • Amazing museums and art studios
